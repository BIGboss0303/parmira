Quality Management

Mira Construction, Palmira and Palmira Group's Quality Management System has been established based on the principles of minimum bureaucracy and documentation in order to be able to make and implement decisions quickly. These features have been achieved through project-based management and process approaches specified below and integrated into the quality management system successfully:

  • Understand the needs of existing and future customers, meet customer needs and make effort to provide services beyond customer expectations,
  • Create and protect an environment enabling employees' full integration in order to realize the company's goals, while leading the way to create a collective purpose and direction,
  • Manage operations and related resources as a single process in order to achieve the desired results more efficiently,
  • Ensure continuous improvement of general performance of our group companies as a permanent goal of the Company organization,
  • Take effective decisions based on analysis of data and information,
  • Establish mutually beneficial relationships with the suppliers in order to create common values.
